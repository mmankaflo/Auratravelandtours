Visa Assistance

Aura Travel & Tours provides professional visa application assistance and guidance to travellers from South Africa. We support tourist, business and student visa applicants with country-specific advice, document checklists, appointment booking guidance and additional travel insurance assistance.

How we help

  • Country-specific guidance — We provide the latest visa requirements for the destination and help you prepare your application.
  • Tourist Visa Support — Documents, invitation letters, travel itineraries and submission guidance.
  • Business Visa Support — Invitation letters, company documentation and appointment assistance.
  • Student Visa Support — Acceptance letters, proof of funds guidance and document preparation.
  • Document Checklist Assistance — We prepare a step-by-step checklist to ensure your application is complete.
  • Appointment Booking Guidance — We advise on scheduling embassy/consulate appointments and provide coaching for interviews when required.
  • Travel Insurance Assistance — Advice and options for policies that meet embassy requirements.

Important disclaimer

"Aura Travel & Tours provides visa application assistance and guidance only. Visa approvals remain at the sole discretion of the relevant embassy, consulate, or immigration authority."

Document checklist examples

Typical documents required (varies by country):

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Completed application form
  • Passport photos (meeting embassy specifications)
  • Proof of accommodation and travel itinerary
  • Return flight reservations
  • Proof of funds (bank statement, payslips)
  • Invitation letter (for business or family visits)
  • Letter of acceptance (for student visas)
  • Travel insurance that meets embassy requirements

Appointment booking guidance

We assist with booking embassy appointments where possible. Appointment availability varies by country and may require advance notice. We provide guidance on preparing documents and what to expect during interviews.
